Learn About The Goal Of This Website

I would like my project to demonstrate the continuum that currency exists on. It will show that virtual world goods have real world value and that the border between economies in a virtual world and a real world are not as clear as they may have appeared at first glance. The audience for this project would be people who are may never have heard about virtual economies and may have no idea what they are. The first deliverable will be exchange rate. It will allow you to just exchange currencies and to compare their values. I will also have a section to explain the goals of the website and what I would like to do with it in the future. The final deliverable will be a website. In addition, another aspect to the website would be a paper that analyses how real world economic laws and principles are applicable in the virtual world. I will point to specific cases may or may not show how specific laws apply in both the real world and virtual world.
